======================
   Specifications
======================

Ethernet
STM32-ARM co-processor
12 PWM
9 DIO
6 Analog I/O
8 Quadrature inputs
8 Pneumatic Solenoid outputs
Wide voltage input, 6v-30v
Reverse polarity protection
UART
I2C
Anderson Power Poles
Feedback LED
Anodized aluminum enclosure
